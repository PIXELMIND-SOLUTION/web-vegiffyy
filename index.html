North Indian

Indulge in the rich, creamy, and spicy flavors of North India. From buttery Dal Makhani to royal Paneer Lababdar and stuffed Amritsari kulchas, every bite is a celebration of tradition and taste.

South Indian

About Ours

At Vegiffyy, we believe vegetarian food is not just a choice — it’s a lifestyle, a celebration of flavors, and a path to health and sustainability. Born out of the passion for pure, plant-based indulgence, Vegiffyy brings you the best of vegetarian cuisines from across India and beyond. From the buttery richness of North Indian gravies to the fiery spice of South Indian curries, from the earthy Rajasthani delights to the tangy flavors of Gujarat — we’ve curated every dish with love and authenticity.
